Why Our Company

Fashion Craft Exports stands as a distinguished name in the realm of fabric manufacturing, specializing in providing a diverse range of Hi-Fashion Export Fabrics. With an illustrious history spanning 26 years, we take pride in our roots deeply embedded in the garment and textile industry.

For five decades, textiles have been the cornerstone of our family business, and we are honored to carry forward this rich tradition. Presently, we supply fabrics to prominent garment exporters in Chennai, Tirupur, Bangalore, Jaipur, and Noida.

Our Features

  • Organic Cotton & Embroideries

    We offer 100% organic cotton fabrics, structural dobby, and intricate over embroideries, combining sustainability with unique textures and designs.

  • Cotton/Lycra & Blends

    Our Cotton/Lycra and Poly/Cotton + Lycra fabrics provide the perfect balance of comfort, flexibility, and durability for shirting and suiting.

  • Denim & Lyocell

    We provide durable 100% denim, versatile Poly Cotton, stretchy Spandex, and eco-friendly Lyocell fabrics for timeless and sustainable garments.

  • Linen & Blends

    Our 100% linen and linen blends provide natural comfort and sophistication, perfect for creating stylish and breathable garments.

Our Process

  • Knitting

    Our advanced knitting process ensures high-quality, durable fabrics with precise textures and consistent patterns. This step is crucial for creating the perfect foundation for all our textile products.

  • Dyeing

    Our dyeing process uses eco-friendly dyes to achieve vibrant, long-lasting colors. We prioritize sustainability while ensuring that each fabric meets our high standards for colorfastness and quality.

  • Cutting

    Precision cutting is essential to maintain the integrity of our designs. Our state-of-the-art cutting techniques ensure accuracy and minimize waste, allowing us to optimize fabric usage efficiently.

  • Printing

    Innovative printing technologies deliver sharp details and rich colors, offering superior quality and customization options for every garment.
